Health Maintenance & Calorie Intake

Maintaining health means balancing calorie consumption with energy expenditure. The average adult requires:

  • Women: ~1,800 to 2,400 kcal/day (depending on activity)
  • Men: ~2,200 to 3,000 kcal/day (depending on activity)

Tracking your meals helps you stay in control of weight management, fuel your workouts, and maintain overall wellness.

Incorporate a variety of nutrient-rich foods (lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, healthy fats) and stay hydrated.
